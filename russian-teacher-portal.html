About the teacher

A linguist who believes every student has their own Russian to discover

I grew up speaking Russian at home and studied linguistics at Moscow State University, graduating with distinction. After a decade of teaching in Moscow and Berlin, I now offer private lessons online and in person across London.

My lessons are never generic. Whether you're learning for travel, business, heritage reconnection, or pure curiosity, I build a programme that fits your pace, goals, and interests.

Russian is famously difficult — and famously rewarding. With the right guide, it becomes one of the most beautiful languages you'll ever speak.
